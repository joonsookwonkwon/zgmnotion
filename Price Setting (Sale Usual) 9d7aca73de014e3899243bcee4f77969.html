Price Setting (Sale/Usual)

Almost all item creators use this strategy but
here in ZGM, you must follow a certain set of rules to use it.
Many ZGM Creators make mistakes here, so please take the following in mind when setting the prices:

Usual, Sale Minimum Price

(Before sale starts) Sale Price

Some templates are set with a specified sale price by the original 3D Creator, and those side notes can be seen from the Item Sheet, in ZEM or Uniqueness & ZEPETO LINKblanks.
In case there aren’t any written, then you can set the sale as low as 2 zem (ZEPETO’s current lowest price).
Otherwise, even the minimum price must be noted when doing sales.

(After sale ends) Usual Price

  • After the sale ends, don’t forget to reset the price back to its Usual price.
    ALL templates have the minimum Usual price,
    and they are also written likewise in the
    ZEM or Uniqueness & ZEPETO LINK blanks.
Maximum Duration + Minimum Gap between Sales

Maximum Duration for one Sale

  • No matter how long the sale is, it must not exceed 7days(1week).
    The encouraged open sale period is usually 24hrs~2days.

Minimum Gap between Sales

  • For an item to have another sale applied, the item must wait in its Usual price
    for
    at least 1 week.
  • Regardless of reasons, consecutive sale for same items is not allowed.
