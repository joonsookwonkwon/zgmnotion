Types of 3D Templates

ZGM has 2 types of 3D item templates, each with their own royalty percentage and advantages.

Range of release3D profits from the 2D royaltiesAdv. for 3D CreatorsAdv. for 2D Creators
P_Templates3D Creator’s shop + ZGM template pool55%Second time profitsAccess to popular templates
O_Templatesonly shared within ZGM template pool60%High profitsMore freedom for texture designs
P_Templates

“Personal Templates”

Templates which have already been released in the 3D Creators’ own shops.

Royalties: Out of the 2D Creator’s royalties, the 55% is 3D Creator’s profits

Advantages💪

3D Creator:

3D Creators can create extra profits out of already used templates, at the same
also get their designs protected according to the design rules.

2D Creator:

2D Creators can use the templates that were up on Hot list or its popularity already proven.

O_Templates✅

“ZGM Original Templates”

ZGM exclusive templates that are not used by the 3D Creator’s own shop.

Royalties: Out of the 2D Creator’s royalties, the 60% is 3D Creator’s profits

Advantages💪

3D Creator:

Since they are templates exclusively made for sharing within ZGM,
We give our 3D Creators higher profits than P_Templates.

2D Creator:

While they are same profit percentage as P_Templates
at the same time no designs used by the 3D Creators,
2D Creators are lesser restricted with designs than P_Templates.
