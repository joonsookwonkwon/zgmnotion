Sales Google Form
Profit Receipt

Approximately 1 month after submitting the Sales Google Form,
NAVER Z sends the total payment+sales data of ZGM Creators to METASPACE-ZGM.

Based on this data, we create receipts for those who were able to withdraw,
and distribute them into the “Individual Drive Folder” alloted for each creators.

After uploading, we inform creators about it in our Discord, via announcements.

Within 3 days after the notice is up,
creators can check their receipts for any errors or clarifications.

If there aren’t any issues,
creators sign the receipt and upload the signed copy on the same folder location.

Sending of Payment

When the 3 day period of checking the receipt is over,
the amount written on the receipt becomes fixed.
Finally, the payments are sent to your Paypal account that you informed ZGM.
For the approximate payment day, please refer to the
Overall Timeline above.
